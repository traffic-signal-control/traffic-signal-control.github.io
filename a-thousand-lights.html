Towards a Thousand Lights

In this paper, we tackle the problem of applying intelligent signal control systems in the real-world.

Traffic congestion plagues cities around the world. Recent years have witnessed an unprecedented trend in research studies on intelligent traffic signal control, both at the individual and region level. However, the primary challenge with real-world impact is to actually deploy an intelligent traffic signal control system. It is not only difficult because of the intelligent traffic signal control syste is an important enabler for the smart cities, but also because there are challenges such as scalability, signal coordination, data feasibility, etc.

Here we present two demos, including:

(1) Large-scale simulative experiments in a roadnet with 2510 signals in Manhattan

(2) Intelligent Signal Control System in Hangzhou
